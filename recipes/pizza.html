Pizza capriciosa

Description

Pizza Capriciosa is a classic Italian pizza with tomato sauce, mozzarella, ham, mushrooms. It is characterized by rich flavor and a variety of ingredients that create a harmonious whole.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 25g fresh yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Additions

  • 3 tbsp tomato paste
  • 150g grated mozzarella
  • 100g canned ham
  • 5 large mushrooms
  • pepper
  • basil
  • oregano
  • granulated garlic

Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk, combine with flour, sugar, salt and oil. Knead into a smooth dough. Roll out into a circle shape, fold the edges to the center.

  2. Spread the pie with concentrate, sprinkle with spices. Arrange one by one: grated mozzarella cheese, sliced: ham, mushrooms and onions.

  3. Bake at 210 degrees for 15-20 minutes, until the edges are browned, enjoy!
